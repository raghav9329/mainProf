This script works as a regular calculator it also is great with stats. When clicking on "more options" new buttons appear, enter your number and then click the "Add" button keep doing so until all numbers are in. Then you can check for min, max, average and even open a window with all the statistics by clicking the "Sta" button.

Simply click inside the window below, use your cursor to highlight the script, and copy (type Control-c or Apple-c) the script into a new file in your text editor (such as Note Pad or Simple Text) and save (Control-s or Command-s). The script is yours!!!
