Dice Poker

Scoring:
3 of a kind: 2 points
4 of a kind: 4 points
5 of a kind: 10 points
Full House (3 + 2 of a kind): 3 points
Low Straight (1,2,3,4,5): 5 points
High Straight (2,3,4,5,6): 6 points
