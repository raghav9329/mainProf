For these meeting requests, please add me, your direct team and any tech/business 
colleagues that you think would need to know of your whereabouts.
 
•	In the Subject line please note what type of out of office situation it is i.e T/C, Business Travel, Sick, Vacation etc.
 
•	In location field please note best Contact Location i.e Phone Nos or External Office, 
        No need to use this if field if you are on vacation

•	Under the ‘Show As:’ drop down, select option Free to make sure that he notification doesn’t block others’ calendar.
 
•	Under ‘Response Options’ drop down, uncheck the ‘Request Responses’ option
